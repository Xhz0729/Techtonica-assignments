Cold Brew Recipe

Cold-brewed coffee is simple to make at home. Just add water to coffee, stir, cover, and leave it out on the counter overnight. This summer, I've been perfecting my cold brew coffee. It's the best drink for summer, and I'd say I'm a cold brew expert, so hear me out!
